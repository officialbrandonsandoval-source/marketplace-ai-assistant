Privacy Policy – Marketplace AI Assistant

Last updated: January 02, 2026

Marketplace AI Assistant is a Chrome extension that drafts reply suggestions for Facebook Marketplace conversations only. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Device identifiers used for authentication.
  • Conversation context required to generate drafts.
  • Usage data for rate limiting and plan enforcement.

How We Use Information

  • Provide AI-generated reply suggestions.
  • Enforce rate limits and plan rules.
  • Operate the service securely and reliably.

Human-in-the-Loop Only

The extension drafts replies only. It does not auto-send messages and does not click the Facebook send button. You must manually send any message.

Credentials

We do not store your Facebook credentials. Authentication is handled by Facebook in your browser session.

Message Content

Message content is processed only to generate draft suggestions for the active Facebook Marketplace thread. The service does not use your messages for advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share data with third parties except service providers required to operate the product (hosting and AI inference).

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate the service, enforce usage limits, and comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

You can request data deletion or ask questions by contacting Brandon@pons.solutions .
