Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Marketplace AI Assistant ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following information solely to provide AI-assisted reply suggestions:

  • Message Content: Text from Facebook Marketplace conversations you are actively viewing, sent to our servers to generate reply suggestions.
  • Listing Information: Title and price of listings associated with conversations, used to provide context-aware suggestions.
  • Account Data: Email address used for authentication and account management.
  • Usage Data: Number of suggestions requested, for rate limiting and service improvement.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect passwords or payment information
  • We do not access messages outside of active Marketplace conversations
  • We do not track browsing history or activity outside Facebook Marketplace
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate Suggestions: Message content is processed by Claude AI (Anthropic) to generate contextual reply drafts.
  • Improve Service: Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns help us improve suggestion quality.
  • Account Management: Email is used for authentication and service communications.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  • Message content is processed in real-time and not permanently stored after suggestion generation
  • Account credentials are hashed and salted
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Processes message content to generate suggestions. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Render: Hosts our backend infrastructure. Subject to Render's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of the service at any time by uninstalling the extension
  • Request a copy of data we hold about you

Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. Suggestion history is retained for 30 days for service improvement, then automatically deleted. You may request immediate deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of material changes via the extension or email.
