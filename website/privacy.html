Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

Marketplace AI Assistant is a Chrome extension that helps users draft responses for marketplace conversations. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Device identifiers used for authentication.
  • Conversation context required to generate drafts.
  • Usage data for rate limiting and billing (plan enforcement).

How We Use Information

  • Provide AI-generated reply suggestions.
  • Enforce rate limits and plan rules.
  • Improve system reliability and support requests.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with service providers needed to operate the product (e.g., hosting, AI inference).

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate the service and comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

You can request data deletion or ask questions by contacting support@marketplace-ai-assistant.com .
